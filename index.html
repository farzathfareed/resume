Objective

I am an entrepreneur with a mission to nurture the entrepreneurial mindset among young individuals, guiding them to set goals for becoming entrepreneurs without incurring debt. In today's world, debt has become a tool for profit, often leading to despair among many small entrepreneurs who initiate or run their businesses on borrowed funds. This burden can result in mental depression, and tragically, in some cases, suicide. Others might resort to unethical measures to repay their debts. The prevailing financial and banking systems have ensnared people in a cycle, constructing an illusory world around them. I believe it's imperative to shift this mindset.
